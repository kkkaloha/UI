Interaction Design Specification

AI-Powered Interview Preparation Assistant — defining the interaction model, information architecture, and navigation structure for a smart adaptive interface (Track A).

🤖Interaction Model

The product adopts a Human-AI Collaborative Interaction paradigm. AI serves as a supportive coach — never the final decision-maker.

🔍

AI Transparency Mode

Every AI output includes an explanation of its reasoning and a confidence indicator. Users can always see why the AI gave a particular score.

Confidence BarsReasoning TracesSource Attribution
🎛️

User Control Mode

Users retain full authority over AI outputs. They can override scores, flag inaccurate feedback, adjust evaluation weights, and undo any action.

Manual OverrideUndo/RedoWeight Adjustment
🛡️

Graceful Degradation

When AI fails (speech errors, incorrect outputs, network issues), the system degrades gracefully with clear fallback options.

Fallback UIError RecoveryLimitation Alerts
ℹ️System Capability Boundaries (Track A Mandatory)
  • ✅ Can: Evaluate answer structure, logical flow, expression clarity, STAR framework usage
  • ✅ Can: Generate role-relevant questions based on JD keywords
  • ❌ Cannot: Judge industry-specific technical depth or domain expertise
  • ❌ Cannot: Replace real interviewer judgment or guarantee outcomes
  • ⚠️ Limited: Voice analysis accuracy depends on audio quality
